Happy Trails –– Hiking Guide

With the increase of outdoor activity as an escape from loneliness and stay-at-home fatigue, hikes have become a popular escape for many people to remain safe and also active. With Happy Trails, we aim to provide the support you need to embark on new journeys every day! With your comfort and convenience in mind, we've created a one stop to access any information you need to be well prepared on any national park hiking trail in the pacific northwest. From the trail's difficulty to figuring out if you can bring your dog along for some fresh air, we want to encourage you to stay healthy and active by providing you with easy access to all the hiking-related information you might ever need!
